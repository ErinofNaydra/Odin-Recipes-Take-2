Dubious Food

Description

Maybe you are low on hearts after mistakingly bumping into a lynel, and all you have to spare are some bokoblin horns, an apple, and a lizalfos claw. Well, you are in luck! Simply throw whatever ingredients you have into the pot and presto you have Dubious Food! A gourmet meal fit for a hero low on health and food options.

Ingredients

  • Monster Parts
  • Anything that could have been edible that will now become barely edible after you cook it with monster parts

Cooking Instructions

  1. Gather any non edible monster part
  2. Gather you soon to be barely edible food item
  3. Heat your cooking pot over a fire and let it get hot for 10 minutes
  4. Throw ingredients in the cooking pot and cook until you hear what sounds like a failed science experiment coming from the pot
  5. Plate any way you like because there is no need for any kind of presentation for this dish.
  6. Attempt to enjoy!
