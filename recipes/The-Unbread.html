The Unbread

This recipe is geared towards innovative minds, chefs, and necromancers alike. If you are looking for a challenge in the kitchen that will threaten the livelihood of your living vegetable kingdom, look no further than our Unbread recipe. Note: We are not responsible for any bodily harm inflicted on unsuspecting parties by the unbred.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a Large Bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm water, and then stir in yeast
  2. Allow to proof until yeast resembles a...rich..creamy...foam *thunder sounds*
  3. Mix in ssalt and oil, then mix in the flour
  4. Knead the dough...cover with a damp cloth
  5. And Allow...to...RISE!!!!
  6. After repeating these words Enjoy your fresh army of The Unbread!
