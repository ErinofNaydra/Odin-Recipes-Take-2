Dragon Pie

If you find time in between running from frogs, spiders, the freezing cold weather, and any other mundane thing that could kill you in the Constant here is a recipe you should try. If you happen to grow a Dragon Fruit in your garden mix it with three twigs and you got yourself a Dragon Pie! Don't ask me how that works, it just does and you need food so who cares? This recipe packs a punch yielding 40 health, 75 hunger, and 5 sanity!

Ingredients

  • 1 Dragon Fruit
  • 3 Twigs

Cooking Instructions

  1. Gather your Sticks and Dragon Fruit
  2. Build a crock pot
  3. Put the twigs in the pot with the dragon fruit
  4. Cook until Dragon Pie Magically Appears
